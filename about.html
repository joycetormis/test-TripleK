About Triple K

Owner: Elaine Dianne Q. Del Rosario

Sole Proprietorship

A full-time mom who loves her family and is dedicated to her business. Triple K, the name of the business was derived from the names of her first 3 children, Khaela, Khael, and Khian, leading to Triple K.

Core Values

PASSION FOR CUSTOMER - Building positive relationships through outstanding service with each interaction.

INTEGRITY - Upholding the highest ethical standards and promoting trust and respect.

EXCELLENCE - Exceeding expectations and taking intense pride in everything we do every day.

LEADERSHIP - Having the courage to rise above challenges, work through adversity, and inspire others.

QUALITY - Delivering high-quality products and services is essential to gaining the trust and loyalty of our clients.

TEAMWORK - Consistently demonstrating an unselfish commitment to working with others to create a collaborative culture.

Mission

To delight our clients in the construction industry by supplying products and services that are best in quality, timely in delivery, and reasonable in cost combined with utmost integrity and care.

Vision

The top choice supplier of products and services in the construction industry in the Philippines.
